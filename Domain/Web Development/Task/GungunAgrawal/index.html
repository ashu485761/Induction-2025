Science Books

Explore the wonders of science through our carefully selected collection

The Theory Of Everything

by Jaico

The Theory of Everything explains the universe’s origin, black holes, and time in simple terms. It’s a brief, accessible take on complex physics.

Understanding the Universe

by Andrew Norton

A clear introduction to key concepts in astronomy and cosmology. It explores how the universe began, evolved, and how we study it today.

Relativity

by Albert Einstein

His groundbreaking theories of space, time, and gravity. It’s a simplified guide to understanding relativity for non-scientists.

Frictional Books

Fictional books transport readers to imaginative worlds, blending creativity and storytelling to explore limitless possibilities.

The Girl in the Glass case

by Devashish Sardana

A gripping psychological thriller where a profiler races against time to catch a twisted serial killer targeting women in Gurgaon.

Ghosts Of The Silent Hills

By Anita Krishan

A chilling collection of real-life and fictional ghost stories set in the eerie landscapes of the Himalayas. The book explores spine-tingling encounters with the supernatural, blending folklore with personal experiences.

by Athur Conan Doyle

A story where Holmes outsmarts a German spy on the eve of World War I. It marks his final case before retirement.
